# The 2026 Customer Acquisition Bible

The synthesis of the 2026 paid acquisition library. Meta, Google, TikTok, low-ticket funnels, SaaS funnels, all in one tactical reference with cross-channel attribution, blended CAC math, AI agents, and 90-day launch plans for SaaS, professional services, and local businesses on $500 to $10,000 monthly budgets.

## What's Inside

- Channel mix decision tree by vertical and budget. The 5x5 grid of channel allocation for SaaS, professional services, and local businesses across budgets from $500 to $10,000 monthly.
- Cross-channel attribution stack: server-side, GCLID + ttclid + fbclid capture, Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM), incrementality testing, first-party data acquisition, modeled conversions reconciliation.
- The acquisition math across all channels: blended CAC, payback period by tier, LTV by vertical, NRR engineering, and the breakeven calculator for paid acquisition stacks.
- AI agents in 2026 acquisition: voice agents for lead qualification, AI lead-scoring, AI ad creative production, AI in outbound. What works, what does not, where to start.
- Three audience swimlane playbooks (SaaS / professional services / local) condensed into one-page references with channel mix, budget split, creative cadence, compliance, and KPI targets.
- The compliance master list: HIPAA, California SB 37 for attorneys, FTC Click-to-Cancel state-level, Stripe chargeback rules, state bar fitness, Meta + Google + TikTok 2026 enforcement updates.
- The 90-day launch plan: week 1 setup, weeks 2 to 4 launch, month 2 scale, month 3 refine. With weekly checklist, KPI targets at each milestone, and the kill rules.
- The 15 most common 2026 acquisition failures and the fix for each, drawn from across the five-channel library.
